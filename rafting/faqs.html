We be all about Families

White Water Pirates was established to accompany families of all sizes and adventure levels. All Families are invited to climb aboard a raft and head out to the open, white water. Will it be rough rapids or smooth streams? Only the River knows fer sure...

We been making Adventures happen fer 10 years

White water Pirates began ten years ago in the great state of California. It be fer those who are unable to take thier family to sea. We make the white water feel like the Ocean. We embark down the Great Sacramento River and ye best have yer wits about.
