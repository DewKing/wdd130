Site Name

white Water Pirates- If ye be Brave or Foolish Procede

Site Purpose and Goals

This website is designed for those who come looking for adventure on the white water. Here you will find the answers to know if white water rafting can be just as adventurous as the open sea. You can explore the different river routes, some wilder for the furious pirates and some calmer for the young buccaneers. As part of this pirate adventure our site goes into adventure extras, like camping on the river bank and even searching for buried treasure. Our site will have examples of maps of the river routes as well as the treasure map you may receive if you go on the camping adventure.

Target Audience

Who might these adventures before? The target audience would be anyone who seeks adventure on the water. It would be good for Families of all sizes. These voyages be not cheap so it may take those who have already found their treasure to pay for these adventures. These adventures will be great for those who enjoy the great outdoors, the smell of the open water and air, and spending time in nature while camping. This site can be accessed by any means necessary, from your desktop to your mobile device. Any Land Lover who seeks a way to leave there world behind can find out how to by visiting this here website.
